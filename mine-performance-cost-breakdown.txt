### **Hidden Savings: The ACH Advantage**
On the new platform, high-ticket items (e.g., $500+ programs) can be paid via **Stripe ACH**.
*   **Statstak ACH Fee:** 1.8% + $2.79
*   **Stripe Direct ACH:** 0.8% (Capped at $5 max)
*   **Result:** Moving a $1,000 payment to ACH saves the owner **~$16 in fees on a single transaction.**
